Hi 👋 I’m Jinsol, a MSCS student of the Computer Science Department in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I have received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University.

My research interests lie in computer systems for processing large-scale workloads in distributed environments. Making edge devices leverage the cloud for scalable inference and learning on stream data, or optimizing communication between distributed devices may be good examples.

I am best reached by email at jinsolpark@snu.ac.kr. Feel free to reach out about my research or anything else I might be able to help with. 😄
